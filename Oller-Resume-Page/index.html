Personal statement

I am a bilingual 17 year old highschool student that is passionate about technology. I am known to be a natural leader and quick on my feet. I use those skills as programming Captain of Haverhill Robotics. Apart from programming in robotics, I also indulge in my own side projects at home. These projects range from silly projects like a game to functions for the robotics team or my computer science class.

I also love to share my knowledge by teaching, which is great because I teach programming to children in my Discovery Club robotics internship, and to new robotics members. When I am not programming I volunteer at Ruth’s house and spend time at home reading about all sorts of things I want to learn about.
